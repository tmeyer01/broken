The World of Puzzles

Puzzles are fun. They bring people together. Young and old alike enjoy their brain teased and twisted. Explore different types of puzzles and find your favorite!

Crypto Puzzles

Decipher, break and penetrate the code. Everyone's favorite especially when a treasure box is involved.

Match Puzzles

Mom said not to play with matches? You can with these matches. All you need to do is to make the equation correct by moving one matchstick. It requires just one click!

Riddle Puzzles

Is riddle a puzzle? The answer is yes! Remember Hermione used a logic to solve a riddle prepared by the potion's master in the first book of Harry Potter?

Word Puzzles

Crossword, word search or Boggles, they are everywhere. But that doesn't mean they are easier. Actually, they could be one of the hardest.

Picture Puzzles

Sometimes it is more about patience and perseverance when it comes to picture puzzles. But it can give you great sense of achievement.
